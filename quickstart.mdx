## Prerequisites

- A Forge account
- A `.lua` or `.luau` file you want to protect

## Protect your first script

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    Sign in to Forge with Google or email \+ password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Obfuscator">
    From the top navigation, select **Obfuscator**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste your code">
    Paste your Lua source into the input editor. Forge supports Lua 5.1–5.4, LuaJIT, and Luau.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a preset">
    Pick a preset like **Balanced**, **Strong**, or **VM Hardened**, or configure individual protection options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build">
    Click **Protect**. Forge runs the pipeline and returns your virtualized output.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Example

Input:

```lua
local function greet(name)
  print("Hello, " .. name)
end

greet("world")
```

Output (truncated):

```lua
return(function()local F=ForgeVM;F:Load("...encrypted payload...");F:Run()end)()
```
