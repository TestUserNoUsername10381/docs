Forge has a free tier and four paid tiers. See the [Pricing page](/pricing) for current prices.

## What plans control

- Monthly **ticket** allowance (see [Tickets](/account/tickets))
- Access to advanced presets (VM Hardened, VM Extreme)
- Hosted script slots
- Watermark removal
- Priority queue
- Premium support

## Plan summary

| Plan | Watermark | VM Presets | Hosted Scripts | Priority Queue |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Free | Yes | VM Standard | 1 | No |
| Starter | No | VM Standard, Hardened | 5 | No |
| Pro | No | All | 25 | Yes |
| Titan | No | All | 100 | Yes |
| Lifetime | No | All | Unlimited | Yes |
