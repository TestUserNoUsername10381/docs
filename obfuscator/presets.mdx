Presets bundle protection options into one click. You can also build custom presets and save them to your account.

## Built-in presets

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Fast" icon="zap">
    Minimal overhead. Variable renaming \+ light string encryption. Ideal for non-sensitive scripts.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Balanced" icon="scale">
    String \+ constant encryption \+ control flow flattening. Recommended default.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Strong" icon="shield">
    Adds dead code, junk instructions, and bytecode mutation.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Maximum" icon="shield-check">
    Every non-VM protection enabled at highest setting.
  </Card>

  <Card title="VM Standard" icon="cpu">
    Full virtualization with a custom Forge VM.
  </Card>

  <Card title="VM Hardened" icon="lock">
    VM \+ anti-tamper \+ integrity verification \+ multi-layer encoding.
  </Card>

  <Card title="VM Extreme" icon="flame">
    Multi-layer VM with mutated dispatch and randomized handler ordering.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Custom" icon="sliders">
    Configure each module individually and save the result.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Comparison

| Preset | Strength | Output Size | Runtime Overhead |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Fast | Low | ~1.2x | ~5% |
| Balanced | Medium | ~3x | ~15% |
| Strong | High | ~6x | ~25% |
| Maximum | Very High | ~10x | ~40% |
| VM Standard | High | ~8x | ~50% |
| VM Hardened | Very High | ~15x | ~70% |
| VM Extreme | Extreme | ~25x | ~100% |

<Note>
  Numbers are approximate and vary by source size and feature mix.
</Note>

## Custom presets

From the Presets page you can:

- Duplicate a built-in preset as a starting point
- Toggle individual protection modules
- Adjust [Output Length](/obfuscator/output-length)
- Save and rename your preset
- Share presets across your account
