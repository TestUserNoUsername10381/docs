Forge appends a watermark comment to all free-tier protected output.

## Free-tier watermark

```text
-- son this was obfs by Forge Obfuscator dang tuff | https://forge.example/
```

## Behavior

- Added automatically on the **Free** plan
- Disabled on **Starter**, **Pro**, **Titan**, and **Lifetime** plans
- The watermark status is shown in the build summary before you select **Protect**
- Removing the watermark from free-tier output is not supported

## Why it exists

The watermark lets free users showcase Forge while signaling that the build was made with the free tier. It does **not** alter your script's behavior or weaken protection.

<Note>
  Watermark text and link are configurable on enterprise plans.
</Note>
